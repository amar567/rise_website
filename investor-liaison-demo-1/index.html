Investor Liaison

Influence and invest

Clear, concise communication is key to maintaining strong investor relations and ensuring a positive investment environment. We perform a pivotal role in facilitating transparent communication between companies and investors. We also provide seed funding to promising startup ventures.

Our services include (but is not limited to):

• Timely updates on grants and funding schemes
• On hand assistance during funding evaluation phases
• Pitch deck and presentation assistance
• Facilitating investor due diligence
